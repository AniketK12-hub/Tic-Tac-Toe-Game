Tic-Tac-Toe

How to Play:

Click on any of the empty squares to place your marker (X or O). The game alternates between players X and O. The first player to get three of their markers in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins the game. If all squares are filled without a winner, the game ends in a tie.

Rules:

  • Players take turns to place their marker.
  • The game ends when one player wins or all squares are filled.
  • Click "Reset Game" to start a new game at any time.
